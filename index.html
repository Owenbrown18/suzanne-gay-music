About

A Salt Spring original

I grew up on Salt Spring Island surrounded by music — and never really left. My background spans jazz, blues, classical, and folk, and I hold a degree in Jazz Performance from Vancouver Island University, but what drives me is simpler than that: finding the moment when music stops feeling like technique and starts feeling like you.

"A sultry voice and emotionally charged performances that stay with you long after the last note."
