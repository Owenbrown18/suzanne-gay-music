Occasions

What Suzanne Performs For

Weddings

From the ceremony walk to the last dance, Suzanne creates an atmosphere of genuine warmth and beauty. Whether you envision soft piano during a garden ceremony or jazz standards at your reception, she tailors every set to your vision.

Funerals & Memorials

Music has a unique power to hold grief and love at the same time. Suzanne brings tenderness, respect, and deep musicality to memorial services — helping families honour a life well lived with sound that truly matches the moment.

Private Parties

Cocktail hours, dinner parties, corporate events, gallery openings — Suzanne sets the room at ease with music that feels alive without demanding attention. The perfect soundtrack for evenings people will remember.
