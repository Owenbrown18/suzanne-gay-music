Weddings

Your wedding,
in sound.

A wedding is a love story told in a single day. The music you choose isn't background — it's memory, woven into every moment from the processional walk to the last slow dance. Suzanne shapes the emotional arc of your celebration with warmth, care, and genuine musicality.

Every set is tailored to your vision: soft solo piano in a garden, acoustic duo for the ceremony, or the full trio filling an evening reception with jazz and soul. Nothing is off-the-shelf.

  • Processional & recessional
  • Signing music & ceremony accompaniment
  • Cocktail hour background music
  • Reception & dinner sets
  • First dance accompaniment

Funerals & Memorials

Honouring a life
with music.

Music holds what words cannot. In grief, sound reaches places that language doesn't — offering comfort, stirring memory, and creating space where loss can be honoured with beauty. Suzanne brings deep tenderness and musical sensitivity to every memorial service.

She can learn songs that carried special meaning to the person being remembered, working with families to ensure the music truly fits the moment. Nothing is treated as routine.

  • Prelude music as guests arrive
  • Processional & recessional
  • Accompaniment during the service
  • Graveside or interment music
  • Reception or gathering afterward

Available Formats

  • Solo — Piano & voice
  • Duo — Guitar & vocals
  • Duo — Piano & vocals

All memorial bookings are handled with care and sensitivity. Please reach out directly to discuss your needs.

Private Parties & Events

The room
comes alive.

The right music transforms a gathering into an event — and an event into a memory. Whether it's a cocktail hour that needs to feel effortless, a dinner party where the music lives just beneath the conversation, or a corporate evening with a jazz trio setting the tone, Suzanne reads the room and plays accordingly.
